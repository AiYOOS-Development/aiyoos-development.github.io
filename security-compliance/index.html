Security by Design

Security & Compliance

AiYOOS Technology Inc.

AiYOOS is a privacy-first AI search engine built on the principle that your data belongs to you. We protect user information through robust security controls and strict privacy practices, earning trust rather than monetizing data.

Security Measures

We implement multiple layers of protection to safeguard your information:

  • End-to-end encryption – All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure infrastructure – Our systems are hosted on audited, certified cloud platforms with continuous monitoring
  • Regular security testing – We conduct ongoing vulnerability assessments and independent security audits to identify and address potential risks
  • Access controls – Strict authentication and authorization protocols limit data access to essential personnel only

Compliance

AiYOOS complies with applicable global and regional data protection regulations, including:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) – European Union
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • CPRA (California Privacy Rights Act)

We handle personal data lawfully, ethically, and transparently, ensuring users maintain control over their information.

Data Privacy Commitment

Our privacy-first approach means:

  • Data minimization – We collect only what's necessary to deliver our service
  • Transparent practices – Clear communication about what data we collect and how it's used
  • User control – You can access, modify, or delete your data at any time
  • No data sales – We never sell user data to third parties

Our Security Promise

"At AiYOOS, security and privacy are not features—they are the foundation of everything we build. Your questions are your business. Your research is your property. Your trust is our responsibility. We exist to answer your questions with accuracy and excellence, not to exploit your curiosity for profit."

For complete details on how we handle your information, please review our full Privacy Policy.
